Screen size is measured using MQtest.io, document.write(screen.width) and document.write(screen.height) in default browsers. If two or more sizes are listed, then that specific device reports different sizes between portrait and landscape mode. In many cases, these measurements don't match the real dimensions of the device. All capable devices will be installed with a recent version of Adobe Edge Inspect.   • Who are we

    DeviceLabPGH is located open space provided by ShowClix. Our mission is to help the developer community in and around Pittsburgh. This lab is set up, and based upon the principles set by ClearLeft and others who have already opened these labs.
